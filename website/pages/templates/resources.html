Dr. Dale Bredesen's work in neurodegenerative diseases, particularly Alzheimer's disease, is groundbreaking and extensive. His books, "The End of Alzheimer's" and its follow-up, "The End of Alzheimer's Program," outline his comprehensive approach to preventing and reversing Alzheimer's. These works detail his protocol, which targets various lifestyle factors and metabolic processes to enhance cognitive function and reverse cognitive decline. The protocol identifies multiple subtypes of Alzheimer's, each with different therapeutic approaches, and has been substantiated in numerous papers, including "Reversal of cognitive decline: A novel therapeutic program" and subsequent studies that showed cognitive improvement in patients following his multifaceted protocol​ (ApoE4 Wiki)​.
