The Data

The dataset below was compiled to ensure strict adherence to the ReCode Protocol and to foster understanding. It originates from daily paper checklists, filled out by family members.

These checklists are being gradually digitized to create this comprehensive digital dataset. It begins in March 2020 and, as of December 2023, is a work in progress.

While we strive for accuracy, there may be errors, and we advise that the dataset be used with caution. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
